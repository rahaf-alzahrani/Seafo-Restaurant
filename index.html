About Us

Before there was Seafo, there was Bill John, a man passionate about making delicious, high-quality seafood available and affordable to everyone, including people who lived far from the coast and regardless of race, gender, religion or economic means. What was once a single, family-owned restaurant in Lakeland, Florida, now has over 100 locations around the world and here we are in Saudi Arabia!
